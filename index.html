Mallick Help Foundation

Mallick Help Foundation can be created with the purpose of improving life in your village by addressing various problems and needs. The foundation’s mission will focus on enhancing local facilities, such as providing water coolers for public use and supporting necessary improvements in the village mosque. This can include repairs, providing equipment, or maintaining the space for the community.

Additionally, if anyone in the village faces an emergency or needs assistance, the Arha Help Foundation could step in to offer help, whether financial or material, based on available resources. The goal of this foundation would be to bring people together and make the village a better, more supportive place for everyone, where any small or big need is met through collective effort.

Mallick Help Foundation

The Mallick Help Foundation aims to improve life in villages by addressing common issues like water supply and maintaining the village mosque. The foundation provides water coolers, assists with repairs and equipment for the mosque, and helps anyone in need within the community.

By bringing the village together, the foundation ensures support in emergencies or when someone requires financial or material aid, making the village a better place for all.
